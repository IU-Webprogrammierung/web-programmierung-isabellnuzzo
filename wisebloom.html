About

The Idea behind the App Design WiseBloom was to combine efficiency with sustainability. The App merges productive task management with environmental impact - It allows users to stay organized while actively contributing to sustainability, regardless of their gardening expertise. It combines three key features;

  • Task Management and To-Do Lists
  • Pomodoro Timer and Sustainable Rewards
  • Group Collaboration for Enhanced Productivity

The interface is designed to feel warm, engaging and personal. All illustrations are hand-drawn by me, to achieve a unique and organic touch that aligns with the app's core values of creativity and sustainability.

By combining structured work techniques, collaborative engagement and tangible ecological impact, the app empowers users to create a more efficient and sustainable future.

Problem Statement

Staying focused and productive is challenging as is and traditional tools lack the right motivation. At the same time, many also want to contribute to sustainability with today's global warming rising by the day but they lack the time and resources. WiseBloom bridges this gap by combining productivity with real-world environmental impact.

Solution

WiseBloom transforms productivity into a purpose-driven experience. By integrating the Pomodoro Technique with real-world environmental action, the app not only keeps the users focused but also rewards their progress with tangible ecological impact. Every completed task contributes to planting trees and supporting pollinator-friendly initiatives, ensuring personal efficiency which will lead to a greener planet.

Walkthrough.

Home
Page

The WiseBloom Home Screen provides a clear and structured overview of daily productivity and environmental contributions. At the top, users are shown their daily goals with the progress being tracked through checkboxes, which encourages focus and accountability. A weekly calendar highlights the productivity milestones, with the current day being prominently marked for easy navigation.

The task list section displays the projects that are currently active, in this case for example "App Design" or "Web Design", alongside progress indicators showing completed versus total tasks. Below, the rewards sections visually represent the user's environmental impact, such as the planting of flowers or trees, earned through completed goals. Users receive on top of that real-world updates, with photos documenting the implementation of their contributions, fostering motivation and transparency.

This interface embodies WiseBloom's mission: transforming productivity into meaningful action by blending task management with tangible ecological rewards.

Lists

WiseBloom Lists Section streamlines task management with an intuitive and visually organized interface. Users can track progress through personalized messages, interactive checklists, and a progress chart for each list. Categories are color-coded for easy navigation, displaying completed versus total tasks at a glance.

The task creation feature allows users to quickly add tasks with a name, due date, description, and category, ensuring efficient organization. Designed for clarity and motivation, this section helps users stay productive and achieve their goals with ease.

Studying

WiseBlooms Studying Screen integrates productivity with structure and motivation. This screen features the Pomodoro Timer, allowing users to focus on tasks like in this example "App Design" in timed intervals. A progress arc visually tracks time spent against the user's goal, while dynamic prompts encourage breaks, like a five-minute pause, to maintain productivity and balance. Intuitive controls —restart, pause, and stop— offer flexibility, while the dropdown menu allows users to switch between task lists seamlessly.

Achievements

The WiseBloom Profile and Achievements Section highlights user progress and tangible contributions to environmental sustainability. In the profile view, users can see their personal details, total learning hours, and an overview of their achievements, including the number of flowers planted for pollinators, bee-friendly meadows created, and trees planted. This visual summary motivates continued engagement while celebrating environmental impact.

The achievements section expands on this, providing updates and evidence of real-world contributions. Users can explore detailed photo galleries showcasing flowers, meadows, and trees planted through their efforts. Each update includes dates, locations, and specific information, such as the planting of marigolds in the Black Forest and their importance to pollinators like bees and butterflies.

This cohesive system fosters transparency and motivation, ensuring users feel connected to the environmental impact of their productivity.

Conclusion

WiseBloom is a purpose-driven platform that integrates productivity with tangible environmental impact. By combining advanced task management tools, the Pomodoro Technique, and progress tracking, it empowers users to enhance focus while contributing to sustainability.

This project highlights how innovative design and functionality can inspire individual growth while addressing global challenges, serving as a model for impactful, future-focused solutions.
