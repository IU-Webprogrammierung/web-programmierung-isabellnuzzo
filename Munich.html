Munich

Concept

LiveClass Munich is a web platform designed to promote student engagement and real-world exploration. By combining gamification and education, it encourages students to discover cultural and hidden gems in their city. Key features include an interactive map with curated locations, customizable avatars, and QR-code-based quizzes that reward students with points. Students can collaborate by purchasing discounted class tickets and track progress on a shared leaderboard, fostering teamwork and healthy competition. This platform bridges the digital and physical worlds, making learning engaging, interactive, and goal-oriented, while inspiring students to connect beyond the classroom.

Problem

In an era dominated by digital interactions, students are increasingly disconnected from real-world experiences and face challenges building meaningful connections outside the classroom. Traditional educational tools often lack the engagement and motivation needed to inspire exploration and teamwork. This leads to limited opportunities for students to connect with peers, discover cultural landmarks, and learn in an interactive, hands-on manner.

LiveClass Munich reimagines education by transforming the city into an interactive classroom. Through an innovative blend of gamification and cultural discovery, the platform inspires students to engage beyond screens and traditional learning environments. With a dynamic map of Munich highlighting cultural landmarks, personalized avatars, and location-based QR-code challenges, LiveClass Munich turns exploration into a rewarding experience. Collaborative tools like discounted group tickets and a class-wide leaderboard foster teamwork and healthy competition, while the gamified structure ensures students remain motivated and engaged. This solution seamlessly bridges education and real-world interaction, offering an inspiring and modern approach to learning that builds connections, curiosity, and a deeper appreciation for the city's cultural fabric.

Homepage

/Homepage

The LiveClass Munich Landing Page serves as the central hub for exploring and engaging with the platform. At its core, it highlights the city of Munich and its cultural, culinary, and recreational offerings through an interactive and visually appealing interface.

A prominent carousel banner showcases key locations and events, inviting users to discover the city's highlights. Below, a calendar seamlessly integrates with the featured activity panel, allowing users to plan visits to landmarks and events, complete with details like meeting points, timings, and tips for participation.

The page further categorizes content into sections such as "Spring in Munich," "Dining in Munich," and "Activities in Munich," offering curated suggestions for seasonal activities, dining options, and local adventures. Each section is enriched with high-quality images and engaging descriptions, accompanied by a "Mehr Entdecken" button to encourage further exploration.

At the bottom, a footer menu provides links to social media channels and essential pages like Terms of Service and Privacy Policy, ensuring easy navigation and accessibility. The home screen is designed to inspire exploration, foster engagement, and provide a user-friendly entry point to the LiveClass Munich experience.

Map

/Map

The Maps section of LiveClass Munich provides an interactive and intuitive way for users to explore popular tours and landmarks across the city. The map is visually engaging, allowing users to view key locations at a glance, marked with precise details to aid navigation.

A dropdown menu enables the selection of specific categories or tours, while filters and a search bar make it easy to tailor the results to individual preferences. The split-screen view combines a detailed map on the right with a list of curated tours on the left, offering essential information such as meeting points, duration, and ticket pricing. Each tour entry includes a distance indicator, ensuring users can plan their visits efficiently.

Designed for clarity and usability, this section bridges the gap between exploration and planning, making it an indispensable tool for students and participants to discover Munich's cultural and historical landmarks with ease.

Tour

/Tour

The Tour section of LiveClass Munich offers a detailed and engaging overview of specific city tours. In this example, the Schloss Nymphenburg tour immerses users in a gamified cultural experience. The screen presents essential tour details, including duration, distance, and accessibility, complemented by a visually rich header featuring images of the landmark.

An interactive storyline invites participants to explore Greek mythology through challenges and tasks, adding an educational and adventurous twist to the tour. The right-hand panel outlines the complete itinerary, with precise stops, distances, and highlights, ensuring users can navigate the experience seamlessly.

Additional tools include navigation support for devices, a tour download option for offline access, and sharing functionality to encourage collaboration and engagement. This section embodies LiveClass Munich's mission to combine education, exploration, and interactivity, creating a dynamic and memorable experience for users.

Leaderboard

/Leaderboard

The Leaderboard section of LiveClass Munich fosters healthy competition and engagement by showcasing the top-performing participants in a dynamic ranking system. This screen highlights the leading students based on completed tours, with the top three achievers prominently displayed on podiums for weekly, daily, or monthly performance.

Each ranking includes participant names, profile pictures, and the number of tours completed, motivating users to stay active and climb the leaderboard. A countdown timer indicates the time remaining until the leaderboard resets, adding a sense of urgency and encouraging continued participation.

Beneath the podium, the broader ranking table provides a detailed overview of all participants, fostering a spirit of inclusion and collective achievement. This section exemplifies LiveClass Munich's emphasis on gamification and collaboration, inspiring students to engage with the platform while celebrating their accomplishments.
